## Event Description

An opportunity for Indian startups and businesses to travel to Australia as part of an Indian delegation to explore business and investment opportunities. Following the trade agreement between India and Australia, organizers partnered with SIPBN to facilitate participation in CAPTECH 2022. The event offered startups the chance to secure investment opportunities worth 10 million AUD, while businesses could form collaborations to expand their networks.
