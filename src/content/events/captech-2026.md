## Event Description

Global opportunities continue to expand, and Punjab Angels Network is proud to once again lead a distinguished India Business Delegation to CAPTECH 2026 Sydney for the 5th consecutive year.

This international platform brings together founders, investors, and industry leaders to engage in meaningful dialogue, explore strategic collaborations, and unlock cross-border investment opportunities. CAPTECH 2026 is designed to foster global partnerships and support businesses looking to scale beyond geographical boundaries.

Through curated networking engagements, high-level discussions, and interactions with global stakeholders, participants will gain valuable insights and connections within Australia's thriving innovation and investment ecosystem.

📍 Sydney | 23rd – 25th September 2026

Hilton Hotel | NSW Parliament House | RSM Australia | Sydney Cricket Ground

📍 Brisbane | 28th September 2026

For entrepreneurs, investors, and business leaders seeking international exposure and expansion opportunities, this delegation provides a powerful gateway to global collaboration and growth.
