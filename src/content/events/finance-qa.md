## Event Description

Every organization has departments like Research & Development, Finance & Accounts, Marketing & Branding, Sales, Legal, HR, and Compliances. While we can be experts in a few areas, we cannot master all of them, leaving some questions unanswered.

This Q&A session addresses finance-related queries. Finance is described as "the blood in an organization"—managing it well can lead to greater heights, while mismanagement can take a toll.

PAN (Punjab Angels Network) is offering expert-led online sessions, virtual tips and tricks, and live networking meetups with industry peers.
