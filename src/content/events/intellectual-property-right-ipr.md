## Event Description

Intellectual property (IP) is the property of your mind or exclusive knowledge. If you develop a new product, service, process or idea it belongs to you and is considered your IP. IP rights provide IP owners with the time and opportunity to commercialize their creations. Establishing a startup is hard but with the right knowledge, the process becomes a little less difficult.

Questions will be entertained after a short deliberation on the topic.
