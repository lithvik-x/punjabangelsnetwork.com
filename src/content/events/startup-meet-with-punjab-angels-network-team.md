# Event: Startup Meet with Punjab Angels Network Team

## Event Details

**Title:** Startup Meet with Punjab Angels Network Team

**Category:** Business Delegation

## Event Description

The Government of Punjab, through Startup Punjab, is organizing a virtual meet with Punjab Angels Network (PAN) to strengthen the startup ecosystem in Punjab. Regional startups, PAN investors, and key advisors will participate.

## Key Speakers

1. **Mr. Raman Bhalla** – President, Sydney Investors (SIPBN); Core Investor
2. **Mr. Ramanan Ramanathan** – Former Mission Director, Atal Innovation Mission; Additional Secretary, NITI Aayog; MD & CEO, CMC Limited; Senior Vice President, TCS
3. **Mr. Srinivasan R. Iynegar** – Director, JBIMS; Strategy Expert
4. **Mr. Sahil Makkar** – Chairman & CEO, Punjab Angels Network; Chartered Accountant; ISB Alumnus
