## Event Description

Punjab Angels Network is the premier platform in India focused on sprinting startups and also building an entrepreneurship ecosystem by advising MSMEs, Corporates and connecting Industries with Academia.

Transform Series is our flagship program, which generally occurs on a quarterly basis, where 2-3 start-ups after the rigorous process are selected for a physical pitch session, along with expert talks, panel discussion on a particular topic, and networking opportunity with investors.
