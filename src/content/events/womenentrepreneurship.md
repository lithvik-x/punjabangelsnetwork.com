## Event Description

The Women Entrepreneurship Ecosystem Development (W.E.E.D) Program is our flagship initiative, specially curated to empower and support women-led startups. Organized on the occasion of International Women's Day, this exclusive event follows a rigorous selection process where 2-3 women-led startups are chosen to pitch their ventures to a panel of investors. The program also features expert talks, an insightful panel discussion on key topics related to women entrepreneurship, and networking opportunities with investors, mentors, and industry leaders to foster growth and collaboration in the ecosystem.

The future of entrepreneurship is inclusive, and W.E.E.D is here to make that a reality.

Be part of this movement and take your entrepreneurial journey to the next level!
